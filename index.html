About Us

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Ms. Septianadrah a dedicated Structural Drafter with over nine years of experience in the architecture, engineering, and construction industry. I specialize in producing high-quality structural models and drawings using Revit, AutoCAD, and Point Cloud data—tools that have become an extension of how I think and work.

In my current role, I report directly to the BIM Modeler Leader and work independently without leading a team. My work has spanned across commercial, industrial, and infrastructure projects, and I take pride in delivering accurate, efficient, and compliant structural designs.

Some of the projects I’m proudest of include modeling bridge and escalator structures for the Pondok Indah Mall renovation, developing precise Revit models for the Data Centre JKT02, and supporting the NCG Groningen Earthquake Assessments with advanced Point Cloud integration. I’ve also contributed to several school infrastructure upgrades, like Ashburton Intermediate, Elermore Vale, and Southland School Projects, ensuring safer and more modern learning environments.

Each project I take on is an opportunity to grow, solve complex problems, and help bring well-designed spaces to life. I consistently meet tight deadlines, adapt to evolving design requirements, and maintain a high standard of precision and coordination.

Now, I’m seeking a new opportunity—one that challenges me, supports my growth, and allows me to continue contributing to meaningful structural work on both local and international scales.
